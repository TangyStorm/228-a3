What Is Leo?

Leo is a next-generation educational AI built for students at every level. Unlike traditional study tools that deliver static content, Leo adapts to your education level and guides you through problems step-by-step.

Leo functions as both a personal tutor and a study management platform, featuring a personal Study Vault and full session history.

Deep Dive

The Problem

Traditional educational software often ignores individual learning curves, leaving students behind or under-challenged. Leo uses adaptive algorithms to personalize lessons in real time.
