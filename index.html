What Is Leo?

Leo is a next-generation educational AI built for students at every level. Unlike traditional study tools that deliver static content, Leo adapts to your education level, guides you through problems step-by-step, and saves key insights to your personal Study Vault automatically.

With real-time streaming responses, file attachment support for vision-based analysis, and a full session history system, Leo functions as both a personal tutor and a study management platform.
